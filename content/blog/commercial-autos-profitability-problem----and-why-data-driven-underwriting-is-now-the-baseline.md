<p id="">The commercial auto market isn’t just cycling through volatility — it’s undergoing a fundamental reset. As <em id="">Carrier Management</em> reported, the commercial auto liability segment posted a <strong id="">113 combined ratio in 2024</strong>, marking <strong id="">14 consecutive years of underwriting losses</strong>. Physical damage remains profitable, but liability headwinds — social inflation, litigation severity, and rising claim complexity — continue to drag down results.</p><p id="">For over a decade, many carriers tried to manage this environment with the same toolkit: static fleet schedules, loss runs, motor vehicle records, and manual audits.
