<p><strong>EYERIDE</strong> and <strong>TruckerCloud</strong> are pleased to announce a new integration that connects <strong>multi-camera video telematics</strong> with <strong>insurance-grade data systems,</strong> enabling faster, smarter decisions across underwriting, risk management and claims.</p>

<p>Through this integration, EyeRide's AI-powered Video Telematics and compliance data are now available within the TruckerCloud ecosystem, making EyeRide an <strong>eligible video and ELD provider</strong> for insurance programs and fleet operators.</p>

<p>As the Telematics industry evolves to <strong>evidence-based ecosystems,</strong> EyeRide's <strong>multi-camera, AI-powered solutions,</strong> combined with TruckerCloud's <strong>insurance data infrastructure,</strong> allows fleets and insurers to operate from a <strong>shared, real-time view of risk and driver safety events.</strong></p>

<h2>What This Integration Delivers</h2>

<h3>For Fleets</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Seamless integration in insurance-driven safety programs</li>
  <li>Accelerated incident resolution and exoneration</li>
  <li>Better visibility into driver risk and operational performance</li>
</ul>

<h3>For Insurance Providers</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Faster FNOL (First Notice of Loss) with instant access to video evidence</li>
  <li>Improved risk scoring and underwriting using real-world driver behavior data</li>
  <li>Reduced claims costs and fraud exposure through verified incident reconstruction</li>
</ul>

<h2>About EyeRide</h2>
<p>EyeRide is an AI-powered video telematics provider delivering multi-camera fleet safety solutions for commercial trucking. EyeRide's platform captures and analyzes driver behavior and road events in real time, giving fleets and insurers the visual evidence they need to resolve incidents faster, reduce liability, and build safer operations.</p>

<h2>About TruckerCloud</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud is the leading telematics data platform for commercial auto insurance. With integrations to over 100 telematics and camera systems, TruckerCloud helps insurers ingest, analyze, and act on fleet data to improve underwriting, claims, and loss control. The platform supports a wide range of fleet types and insurance applications.</p>
