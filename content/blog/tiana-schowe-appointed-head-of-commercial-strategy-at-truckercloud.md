<p id="">ATLANTA, July 22, 2025 -- TruckerCloud is pleased to announce that Tiana Schowe, CPCU, has joined the company as Head of Commercial Strategy. Schowe brings two decades of experience in the transportation insurance sector, which will be instrumental in driving TruckerCloud's mission to help the commercial auto insurance industry lower loss ratios by providing telematics insights at scale.</p><p id="">"I've long admired TruckerCloud's products and ingenuity, and I'm thrilled to officially join the team," commented Schowe. "Their focus on delivering practical, impactful solutions and data aligns perfectly with my passion for leveraging technology to create real value for businesses."</p><p id="">In her new role, Schowe will be pivotal in shaping TruckerCloud's commercial direction, identifying new market opportunities, and ensuring the company's offerings continue to meet the evolving needs of the transportation sector.</p><p id="">TruckerCloud CEO Spencer Mitchell expressed enthusiasm about Schowe's appointment, stating, "Tiana's deep understanding of the transportation landscape and her proven track record in driving successful strategies make her an invaluable addition to our team. Her insights will be crucial as we continue to innovate and expand our reach, solidifying TruckerCloud as the industry leader in intelligent solutions."</p><p id="">TruckerCloud's platform equips insurance underwriters, loss control teams, and claims organizations with advanced operational insights to improve risk assessment and management across commercial auto.</p><p id="">Schowe added, "What sets TruckerCloud apart is its foundation in genuine industry understanding and market saturation. They have successfully integrated with the majority of telematics and camera providers and, as a result, are working with a large portion of the transportation insurance industry. I'm excited to contribute to a company that truly empowers safety and transparency in the transportation industry."</p><h5 id="">About TruckerCloud</h5><p id="">TruckerCloud is the leading telematics data platform for commercial auto insurance. With integrations to over 100 Electronic Logging Device (ELD) and camera systems, it enables insurers to launch telematics-based programs with turnkey ease. Purpose-built for the industry, TruckerCloud offers tools for enhanced risk assessment, streamlined claims reporting, and effective loss control, transforming commercial auto insurance with greater transparency, efficiency, and data-driven decision-making.
