<p>Follow these steps to connect your Lucid ELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Lucid ELD account and click the <strong>Settings</strong> gear icon in the bottom-left corner</li>
  <li>Under the <strong>Developer</strong> section, click <strong>API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Create New</strong> in the top-right corner</li>
  <li>Type <strong>TruckerCloud</strong> for the API key note, then click <strong>Create</strong></li>
  <li>Your new API key appears at the top of the list — click the copy icon to copy it</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste the API key, then click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
