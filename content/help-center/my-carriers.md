<p>The <b>My Carriers</b> page is your central hub for managing all fleets connected to your TruckerCloud account.</p>
<h2>Onboarded Carriers</h2>
<p>The main table shows all fleets that have completed onboarding. For each carrier, you can see carrier name, status (Active, Paused, Disconnected), Policy ID, and Customer Unique ID.</p>
<h2>Invited Carriers</h2>
<p>The <b>Invited Carriers</b> tab shows fleets that started the onboarding widget but did not complete it. Useful for follow-up.</p>
<h2>Terms &amp; Conditions</h2>
<p>You can download a PDF of the T&amp;C for any onboarded carrier, stamped with the signer's name, IP address, and timestamp.</p>
<h2>Adding Metadata</h2>
<p>Click edit on any carrier row to add a <b>Policy ID</b> or <b>Customer Unique ID</b> (quote ID, internal reference, notes).</p>
<h2>Pausing Fleets</h2>
<p>Pausing stops data collection and risk score calculation for a fleet. Common reasons: policy cancelled, fleet no longer a customer. Pausing is reversible — reactivate at any time.</p>
