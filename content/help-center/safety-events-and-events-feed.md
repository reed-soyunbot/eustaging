<p>TruckerCloud captures safety events from connected ELDs and cameras through two views: the <b>Safety Events Dashboard</b> (fleet-level summary) and the <b>Events Feed</b> (event-level detail with video and route data).</p>
<h2>Safety Events Dashboard</h2>
<p>Fleet-level roll-up designed for loss control. Shows event type distribution, event source (inward/outward camera or ELD/accelerometer), event trends over time, events by time of day, and events by location.</p>
<h2>Events Feed</h2>
<p>Detailed event-level view showing the most recent 1,000 events. Search by carrier, VIN, or event type. Click into any event to see speed, g-force, vehicle/driver details, video footage, and route.</p>
<h2>Route Replay</h2>
<p>Click <b>"View Route"</b> to see the route plotted on a map. Blue = normal speed; colored lines = speeding severity; black = 20+ MPH over the limit. Especially useful for claims investigations.</p>
<h2>Accelerometer vs. Camera AI Events</h2>
<ul><li><b>Accelerometer events</b> (harsh brakes, corners, accelerations, high G-force) — included in the Risk Score</li><li><b>Camera AI events</b> (lane departure, tailgating, distracted driving) — shown for informational purposes only, NOT included in the Risk Score</li></ul>
<h2>Crash Notifications</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud sends push notifications specifically for crash events so claims teams are alerted immediately.</p>
