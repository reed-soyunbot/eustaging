<p>Follow these steps to connect your Transflo account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter the administrator username and password from your Transflo ELD portal</li>
  <li>Click <strong>I agree to terms and conditions</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Complete Sign Up</strong></li>
</ol>
