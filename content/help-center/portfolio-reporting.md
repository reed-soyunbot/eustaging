<p>Portfolio Reporting gives you a cross-fleet view of your entire TruckerCloud account.</p>
<h2>Portfolio Mileage Reporting</h2>
<p>View total mileage across all fleets with a custom date range. Export for billing or audit. For usage-based programs, set a weekly or monthly timeframe and export miles by fleet to calculate premiums.</p>
<h2>Portfolio Location Reporting</h2>
<p>Interactive map showing the current location of every vehicle across all your fleets. Useful for quick visual checks — e.g., flagging unexpected activity in Mexico.</p>
<h2>Portfolio Heartbeat</h2>
<p>High-level view of fleet connectivity: carrier name, status (Active/Paused/Disconnected), last risk score date, credentials validated date, last fleet activity, total assets, and total active trucks.</p>
<h2>Portfolio Fleet Miles by Zip</h2>
<p>Combined miles-by-zip-code across all fleets — a book-of-business geographic exposure view.</p>
