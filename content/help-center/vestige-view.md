<p>Follow these steps to connect your Vestige View account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Email <a href="mailto:support@vestigeview.com">support@vestigeview.com</a> and ask them for a TruckerCloud Client ID and Client Secret</li>
  <li>When you receive the values, copy them</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste the Client ID and Client Secret in the fields</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
