<p>Follow these steps to connect your ATT account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your ATT Administrator account</li>
  <li>Create a new user in your ATT account</li>
  <li>Set the username to <strong>canal@"databasename"</strong> where "databasename" comes from your web portal URL</li>
  <li>Your database name is the path component of your portal URL. For example, in <code>https://my258.geotab.com/abc_company/</code>, the database name is <strong>abc_company</strong> and the server is <strong>my258</strong></li>
  <li>Once credentials are created, return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter the new username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
