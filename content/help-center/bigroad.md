<p>Follow these steps to connect your BigRoad account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your BigRoad account at <a href="https://app.bigroad.com">https://app.bigroad.com</a></li>
  <li>Click on the <strong>Fleet</strong> tab</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Apps</strong> on the left</li>
  <li>Click the button to generate an API key</li>
  <li>Copy the generated API key</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the key</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
