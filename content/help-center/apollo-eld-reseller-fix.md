<p>If your Apollo ELD API key fails verification on the Apollo ELD onboarding tile, your device is most likely being resold by another provider. Follow the steps below.</p>
<h2>Why this happens</h2>
<p>Apollo ELD is a white-label platform. Many fleets buy Apollo hardware through resellers — most commonly <b>OneStepGPS, GPSTrackit, or Linxup</b>. The reseller issues a different API key from a different portal, and that key only works on the reseller's onboarding tile, not the Apollo ELD tile.</p>
<h2>How to fix it</h2>
<ol><li>Email your reseller (OneStepGPS, GPSTrackit, or Linxup) and request an API key. Specify clearly: "I need an API key to share data with TruckerCloud."</li><li>When you receive the key, return to TruckerCloud onboarding.</li><li>Search for and select the <b>OneStepGPS, GPSTrackit, or Linxup tile</b> — whichever matches your reseller. Do <b>not</b> use the Apollo ELD tile.</li><li>Paste the key and submit.</li></ol>
<h2>Still stuck?</h2>
<p>If you are unsure who resells your Apollo device, check the branding on your portal login page or your hardware invoice, then contact <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a>.</p>
