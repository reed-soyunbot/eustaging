<p>Follow these steps to connect your Intellishift account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Intellishift account</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Administration</strong> on the left sidebar</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Add User</strong> in the top right corner</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Start From Scratch</strong></li>
  <li>Fill in: email (any), First Name: <strong>API</strong>, Last Name: <strong>Truckercloud</strong>, User Type: <strong>Administrator</strong></li>
  <li>In <strong>Assign Branches</strong>, click <strong>Select All</strong></li>
  <li>In <strong>Permissions</strong>, assign All to <strong>Telematics</strong>, toggle on <strong>Connect API</strong>, and toggle on <strong>AI Dash Cam</strong> (Read), then click Create User</li>
  <li><strong>Note:</strong> If you don't have Connect API, contact Intellishift to have it added to your account</li>
  <li>Set up the password for the new user via the invitation email</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Enter the new username (email) and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
