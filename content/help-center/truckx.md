<p>Follow these steps to connect your TruckX account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your TruckX account</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Share API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Search for and select <strong>TruckerCloud</strong></li>
  <li>Select <strong>all drivers</strong>, <strong>all trucks</strong>, and <strong>all trailers</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Send API Key</strong></li>
</ol>
