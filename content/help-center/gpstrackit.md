<p>Follow these steps to connect your GPSTrackit account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your <a href="https://cloud.gpstrackit.com/login">GPSTrackit account</a></li>
  <li>On the left sidebar, click <strong>Account</strong></li>
  <li>At the top of the page, click <strong>API Keys</strong></li>
  <li>Click the <strong>+</strong> to add an API key</li>
  <li>Name the key and press <strong>Create</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the generated API key</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the key</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
