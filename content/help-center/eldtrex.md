<p>Follow these steps to connect your ELDTrex account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Access the <strong>ELD Fleet Portal</strong> using your carrier credentials</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Settings</strong> in the left-hand menu</li>
  <li>Select <strong>API</strong> from the top menu</li>
  <li>Click the <strong>Add</strong> button on the right side</li>
  <li>Fill in the form:
    <ul>
      <li><strong>Name:</strong> client application name</li>
      <li><strong>Status:</strong> ACTIVE</li>
      <li><strong>Scopes:</strong> ALL</li>
      <li>Enable the checkbox for dashcam integration</li>
      <li>Enable the checkbox for GPS trackers (trailer tracking)</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Click <strong>Save</strong> — the API key will appear in the table (e.g., xx-1234567)</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste the API key, then click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
