<p>Follow these steps to connect your NexTraq account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Email your NexTraq account manager and ask for API credentials</li>
  <li>They will send you a DocuSign — sign it and it will be automatically returned to them</li>
  <li>NexTraq's API support team will generate and email you your credentials</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
