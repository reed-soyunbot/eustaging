<p>Follow these steps to connect your Orbcomm account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Orbcomm account at <a href="https://www.fleetmanager.com/#/login">https://www.fleetmanager.com/#/login</a></li>
  <li>Copy your Orbcomm credentials (email and password)</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter your username and password in the required fields</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Complete Sign Up</strong></li>
</ol>
