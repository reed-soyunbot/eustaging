<p>Peoplenet requires a data consent form to be completed before connection. Follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Complete the <a href="https://na4.docusign.net/Member/PowerFormSigning.aspx?PowerFormId=118c4bad-6167-4799-b40f-34d9dc37952c&env=na4&acct=dcc58713-002d-4605-86a9-9be5e3f4bf55&v=2">PeopleNet Data Access Form</a></li>
  <li>TruckerCloud will submit the form to PeopleNet</li>
  <li>Within 1–2 business days, PeopleNet will provide credentials to complete the connection</li>
</ol>
