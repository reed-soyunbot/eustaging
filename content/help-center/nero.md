<p>Follow these steps to connect your Nero account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Contact your Nero Account Manager to request that the API be enabled for your account</li>
  <li>Create a separate User Group that has access to the API and the <strong>Data Exchange Access</strong> permission (e.g., name it "API Users")</li>
  <li>Click <strong>User</strong> in the left menu and create a new user, selecting the "API Users" group</li>
  <li>When you click <strong>Invite</strong>, an email is sent prompting you to set a password</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Authorize</strong> and enter the new username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
