<p>Follow these steps to connect your Forward Thinking Systems account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Send an email to <a href="mailto:support@ftsgps.com">support@ftsgps.com</a> CC'ing <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a></li>
  <li>Use the subject: <strong>(Customer Name) - TruckerCloud Integration Request</strong></li>
  <li>In the body: <em>"(Customer Name) is requesting an integration with TruckerCloud. Please generate REST API Credentials with access to the FleetCam and Intellihub APIs for this customer."</em></li>
  <li>They will email you a new username and password</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Enter the username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
