<p>Mileage reports are used for insurance underwriting, renewals, exposure analysis, and audits.</p>
<h2>Accessing Mileage Reports</h2>
<p><b>For a specific carrier:</b> Select the carrier, then navigate to <b>Mileage Reporting</b>. The report defaults to the previous month.</p>
<p><b>For your entire portfolio:</b> Stay on the main dashboard (no carrier selected), then navigate to <b>Mileage Reporting</b> to see aggregated mileage across all carriers.</p>
<h2>Odometer Miles vs. GPS Miles</h2>
<ul><li><b>Odometer Miles</b> — Pulled from the vehicle's ECM using start/end odometer readings. Most accurate; preferred for underwriting and audits.</li><li><b>GPS Miles</b> — Calculated from GPS location pings. Available even when odometer data is missing, but less precise.</li></ul>
<p>Use odometer miles when available. Use GPS miles as a secondary reference when odometer data is missing.</p>
