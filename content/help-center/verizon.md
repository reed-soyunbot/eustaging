<p>You will need to install the TruckerCloud application in the Verizon Reveal Marketplace; then you'll be emailed a username and password to enter in the onboarding fields.</p>
<h2>Steps to Connect</h2>
<ol><li>Log into your Verizon Reveal account: https://reveal.fleetmatics.com/login.aspx</li><li>Navigate to the Reveal Marketplace</li><li>Search for the <b>TruckerCloud</b> partner tile and click <b>Request Integration</b></li><li>Follow the instructions to sign the Data Consent</li><li>Once you receive a username and password via email, enter them into the Verizon tile fields</li><li>Click <b>Authorize</b>, then <b>Done</b></li></ol>
<p><b>If credentials fail:</b> Make sure ALL your vehicles and drivers have been assigned Names and Numbers in your Verizon account, with no special characters in those fields.</p>
<p>See the <a href="/help-center/verizon-connect-reveal-onboarding-faq">Verizon Connect Reveal Onboarding FAQ</a> for detailed troubleshooting.</p>
