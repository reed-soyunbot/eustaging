<p>Follow these steps to connect your Eyeride account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to the Eyeride Portal</li>
  <li>Scroll down the left sidebar and select <strong>Administration</strong> &rarr; <strong>Users</strong></li>
  <li>Click the <strong>+</strong> button to create a new user</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Manual User Creation</strong></li>
  <li>Fill in the required fields including a username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Save</strong>, then scroll down to edit the user</li>
  <li>Assign <strong>Company Admin</strong> status to the user</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Enter the new username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
