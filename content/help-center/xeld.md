<p>Follow these steps to connect your XELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your XELD account at <a href="https://cloud.xeld.us/#/auth/login">https://cloud.xeld.us/#/auth/login</a></li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Admin</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
  <li>Click <strong>API</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>+ Add API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Name the key <strong>Insurance</strong> and set permissions to <strong>Read-Only</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the generated API key</li>
  <li>Paste it into the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
</ol>
