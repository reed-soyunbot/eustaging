<p>Follow these steps to connect your Chronos ELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Chronos ELD system</li>
  <li>Go to the carriers list</li>
  <li>Copy the API key from your carrier</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the key</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
