<p>Follow these steps to connect your Peak ELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Peak ELD account</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Admin</strong> in the left sidebar</li>
  <li>Click <strong>API</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>+ Add API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Set permissions to <strong>Read-Only</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the generated API key</li>
  <li>Paste it into the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
</ol>
