<p>The Radius of Operations dashboard shows how far each vehicle travels from its garaging zip code. This helps validate whether a fleet is operating locally, regionally, or long-haul.</p>
<h2>How Garaging Zip Is Determined</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud automatically infers each vehicle's garaging zip based on where the vehicle is most frequently located at <b>3:00 AM</b> over the last 30 days.</p>
<h2>Overriding the Garaging Zip</h2>
<p>Override at the individual vehicle level or in bulk. A country code is required (US and Mexico share some zip code formats). Radius calculations recalculate immediately after an override.</p>
<h2>Radius Slider</h2>
<p>Use the radius slider to filter and visualize how many vehicles operate within a certain distance of their garaging zip. Identify outlier vehicles that travel significantly farther than the rest of the fleet.</p>
