<p>Follow these steps to connect your VLV (Omnitracs) account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>If you do not have an Omnitracs VLV account, the carrier company admin must sign in to review the VLV terms of service:
    <ul>
      <li><a href="https://customer.omnitracs.com/legal/vlv">US Customers</a></li>
      <li><a href="https://customer.Omnitracs.ca/legal/vlv">Canadian Customers</a></li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Within one business day, you will receive an email with a link to the VLV portal along with a username and password</li>
  <li>Log in to the <a href="https://vlv.omnitracs.com">VLV portal</a></li>
  <li>Find <strong>Tranzlogix</strong> in the permissions list</li>
  <li>Set <strong>Add My Fleet to Active VLV Carrier List</strong> to <strong>Yes</strong></li>
  <li>Set <strong>Authorize Customer to Track My Fleet</strong> to <strong>On</strong></li>
  <li>You will be emailed credentials — return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and enter them</li>
</ol>
