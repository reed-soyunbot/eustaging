<p>Follow these steps to connect your ELD Mandate account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your ELD Mandate account at <a href="https://www.eldmandate.biz/hos">https://www.eldmandate.biz/hos</a></li>
  <li>On the left side, click <strong>Settings</strong> then <strong>Users</strong></li>
  <li>In the upper right of the screen, click the <strong>+</strong> to add a new user</li>
  <li>Fill in the following:
    <ul>
      <li>First Name: <strong>Insurer</strong></li>
      <li>Last Name: <strong>Integration</strong></li>
      <li>Email: insurer@[yourcompany].com</li>
      <li>Password: choose a password</li>
      <li>Available User Roles: select <strong>USER_ROLE_USERADMIN</strong></li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Copy the new username and password</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
