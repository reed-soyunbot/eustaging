<h2>Steps to Connect</h2>
<ol><li>Find <b>API Management</b> from the menu in your Rollingtrans account.</li><li>Click the button to generate the key.</li><li>Click the <b>TruckerCloud Token</b> tab.</li><li>Click <b>Create Token</b>.</li><li>Click <b>Copy</b> when the token is generated.</li><li>Return to the onboarding portal, click <b>Authorize</b>, paste the token, and click <b>Verify Credentials</b>.</li><li>Click <b>Done</b>.</li></ol>
