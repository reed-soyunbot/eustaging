<p>Follow these steps to connect your Air ELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Air ELD account at <a href="https://app.aireld.com/login">https://app.aireld.com/login</a></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Manage</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>API Keys</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>+ Generate API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Leave the Expiration Date <strong>blank</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the generated key</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste the API key in the field</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
