<p>Follow these steps to connect your DriverTech account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Email <a href="mailto:WebServiceSupport@DriverTech.com">WebServiceSupport@DriverTech.com</a> requesting API access credentials</li>
  <li>Include your <strong>CompanyCode</strong>, which is displayed at the top right of your Customer system management Web Portal</li>
  <li>Once you receive credentials, return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
