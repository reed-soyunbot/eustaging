<p>Follow these steps to connect your JJ Keller account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Email <a href="mailto:tsimplementation@jjkeller.com">tsimplementation@jjkeller.com</a> CC'ing <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a>, requesting API credentials for TruckerCloud integration</li>
  <li>Include in the email:
    <ul>
      <li>Carrier Name</li>
      <li>Carrier DOT</li>
      <li>Contact information</li>
      <li>Customer name and email address</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>A JJ Keller representative will contact you to complete a data sharing agreement</li>
  <li>Once you receive credentials, return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
