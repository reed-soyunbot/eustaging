<p>Samsara uses OAuth to connect to TruckerCloud. Follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
  <li>In the TruckerCloud onboarding widget, click <strong>Authorize</strong> next to Samsara</li>
  <li>You will be redirected to Samsara — log in as an <strong>admin</strong></li>
  <li>Select your <strong>parent organization</strong> (not a sub-org)</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Install</strong> to grant TruckerCloud access</li>
</ol>
<p>Once authorized, TruckerCloud will automatically pull your fleet data from Samsara.</p>
