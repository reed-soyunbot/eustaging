<h2>What TruckerCloud Helps You Do</h2>
<ol><li><b>Understand risk</b> — Through risk scores, mileage data, geographic analysis, and safety events</li><li><b>Price risk</b> — Using real driving data instead of application estimates</li><li><b>Improve risk</b> — Through loss control insights and fleet monitoring</li><li><b>Claims</b> — Receive crash video footage and contextual data within minutes</li></ol>
<h2>Platform Navigation</h2>
<p>When you log in, you land on the <b>Portfolio Dashboard</b> showing aggregate Risk Score and key metrics across all fleets. Click into any fleet to access fleet-level dashboards: Summary Report, Heartbeat, Mileage, Geographic Risk, Radius of Operations, Safety Events, Events Feed, and Drivers.</p>
<h2>Exporting Data</h2>
<p>Look for the <b>three-dot menu (⋯)</b> on any table or chart to download as Excel. The Summary Report also has a PDF export.</p>
<h2>Key Concepts</h2>
<ul><li><b>TSPs</b> — TruckerCloud connects to ELD, camera, and GPS providers. Data flows automatically once a fleet completes onboarding.</li><li><b>Time Zones</b> — Most timestamps are displayed in UTC (5 hours ahead of US Eastern).</li><li><b>Historical Data</b> — Up to one year of historical data is pulled when a fleet onboards.</li></ul>
