<p>Telematics Visibility lets you control which telematics vendors appear in your carrier onboarding widget.</p>
<h2>How It Works</h2>
<p>By default, all telematics vendors are available. Once you edit the list, it switches from automatic mode to manual mode — any new providers added to the platform in the future must be manually added by you.</p>
<h2>Removing a Vendor</h2>
<ol><li>Go to <b>Settings &rarr; Telematics Visibility</b></li><li>Click <b>Add Telematics Providers</b></li><li>Start with <b>Select All</b>, then unselect the vendor(s) you want to remove</li><li>The unselected vendor(s) will no longer show in the onboarding widget</li></ol>
<h2>Adding a Vendor Back</h2>
<ol><li>Go to <b>Settings &rarr; Telematics Visibility</b></li><li>Click <b>Add Telematics Providers</b></li><li>Select the vendor to restore and click <b>Add</b></li></ol>
<p><b>Note:</b> Removing a vendor from the widget does not disconnect existing telematics integrations.</p>
