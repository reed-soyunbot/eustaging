<p>Motive uses OAuth to connect to TruckerCloud. Follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
  <li>In the TruckerCloud onboarding widget, click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>You will be redirected to a Motive login page — log in as an <strong>administrator</strong></li>
  <li>You will be taken to your insurer's Motive App</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Install</strong></li>
</ol>
<p>Once authorized, TruckerCloud will automatically pull your fleet data from Motive.</p>
