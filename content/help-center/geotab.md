<p>Follow these steps to connect your Geotab account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Geotab Administrator account</li>
  <li>Create a new user with permissions set to <strong>Admin</strong> or <strong>View Only</strong></li>
  <li>Name the user in the format <strong>IntegrationName_User</strong> (e.g., <strong>Insurer_User</strong>)</li>
  <li>Once credentials are created, return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter the new username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
