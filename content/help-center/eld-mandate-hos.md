<p>Follow these steps to connect your ELD Mandate HOS account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your ELD Mandate admin account at <a href="https://www.eldmandate.biz/hos">https://www.eldmandate.biz/hos</a></li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Settings</strong>, then <strong>Edit Profile</strong></li>
  <li>Copy your API Key</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste the API key in the field</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
