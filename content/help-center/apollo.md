<p>Follow these steps to connect your Apollo account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<p><strong>If your Apollo ELD was purchased through a reseller</strong> (OneStepGPS, GPSTrackit, or GPS Insight), use those provider's onboarding instructions instead.</p>
<p><strong>If you purchased Apollo directly</strong>:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Contact Apollo directly and ask them to generate an API key for you</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter the API key in the required field</li>
  <li>Click <strong>I agree to terms and conditions</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Complete Sign Up</strong></li>
</ol>
