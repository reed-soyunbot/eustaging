<p>This guide walks you through the step-by-step process for onboarding your fleet and sharing telematics data with your insurance provider.</p>
<h2>Step 1: Review the Program &amp; Eligible Providers</h2>
<p>When you begin onboarding, you'll see an overview page explaining the program. Review the list of eligible camera and ELD/telematics providers, then click <b>Onboard</b> to get started.</p>
<h2>Step 2: Complete the Initial Setup Form</h2>
<p>Fill in the required information (such as your DOT number) and click <b>Onboard</b> to continue.</p>
<h2>Step 3: Select Your Provider</h2>
<ol><li>Search for your telematics or ELD provider</li><li>Click the provider's logo</li><li>Follow the step-by-step guide that appears on screen</li></ol>
<h2>Step 4: Follow Provider-Specific Instructions</h2>
<ul><li><b>OAuth 2.0</b> (e.g. Samsara, Motive) — Click Authorize, agree to terms</li><li><b>Username &amp; Password</b> (e.g. Azuga, Geotab) — Enter credentials from your provider account</li><li><b>Marketplace Authorization</b> (e.g. Verizon) — Install TruckerCloud from their marketplace first</li><li><b>API Key</b> (e.g. BigRoad) — Generate and paste an API key from your provider's Apps section</li><li><b>DocuSign</b> (e.g. Omnitracs) — Complete a DocuSign form, then forward the signed copy to your Omnitracs account manager</li></ul>
<h2>Step 5: Confirm &amp; Finish</h2>
<p>Once your credentials are verified, click <b>Done</b>. If you have additional providers, continue onboarding them one at a time.</p>
