<h2>Steps to Connect</h2>
<ol><li>Log into your ILog ELD account and click the <b>Settings</b> icon (gear) in the bottom-left corner.</li><li>Click <b>API Key</b> under the Developer section. Click <b>Create New</b>.</li><li>Type "TruckerCloud" for the note, then click <b>Create</b>.</li><li>Click the copy icon next to the new key to copy it.</li><li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding screen and click <b>Authorize</b>.</li><li>Paste the API key and click <b>Verify Credentials</b>, then <b>Done</b>.</li></ol>
<p>If you have any questions, use the chat icon in the bottom right or contact your account manager.</p>
