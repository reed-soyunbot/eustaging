<h2>What Is TruckerCloud?</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud is a telematics platform built for commercial auto insurance. It integrates with 100+ telematics service providers (ELDs, GPS trackers, dash cameras), standardizes data across providers, and analyzes it for insurance underwriting, loss control, and claims.</p>
<h2>Fleet Consent &amp; Onboarding</h2>
<p>All data is collected only with fleet consent. Fleets onboard through the TruckerCloud portal and securely share their telematics credentials.</p>
<h2>Risk Score</h2>
<p>The Risk Score is calculated using harsh driving events, speeding behavior, and geographic risk exposure — on a rolling 30-day basis.</p>
<h2>Mileage Reporting</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud calculates both <b>odometer miles</b> (from vehicle ECM) and <b>GPS miles</b> (from location pings). View mileage at the fleet or vehicle level, by month, or export to Excel.</p>
<h2>Geographic Analysis</h2>
<p>View miles driven by zip code, county, or state. Heat maps show mileage density. Useful for identifying territorial exposure.</p>
<h2>FNOL (First Notice of Loss)</h2>
<p>When a potential crash is detected, TruckerCloud sends notifications via email or API with a crash video download link and a PDF report containing vehicle info, driver info, speed, location, and event details.</p>
