<p>Follow these steps to connect your Pedigree account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Pedigree portal at <a href="https://www.pedigreetechnologies.com/login/">https://www.pedigreetechnologies.com/login/</a></li>
  <li>Click the <strong>Admin</strong> tab, select <strong>Users</strong>, then click <strong>Create New User</strong></li>
  <li>Set permissions to <strong>Admin</strong> or <strong>View Only</strong></li>
  <li>Name the user in the format <strong>IntegrationName_User</strong> (e.g., <strong>Insurer_User</strong>)</li>
  <li>Once credentials are created, return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter the new username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
