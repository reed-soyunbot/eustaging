<p>If you see the error <b>"Contact Email is Invited by Another Company"</b> when clicking Onboard, follow the steps below.</p>
<h2>Why this happens</h2>
<p>You (or someone using the same email) previously started onboarding with one set of information, and the new attempt does not match exactly. Even a difference in case or an extra space will trigger this error.</p>
<h2>How to fix it</h2>
<ol><li>Enter exactly the same email, company identifier, first name, and last name you used previously.</li><li>If you cannot replicate the previous entry, add a <b>+1</b> to your email address (e.g. <code>support+1@truckercloud.com</code>). The email will still reach you.</li><li>If +1 is also taken, increment it: <code>support+123@truckercloud.com</code>.</li></ol>
<h2>Still stuck?</h2>
<p>Contact us via the chat icon or <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a> and we will reset the prior invitation on our side.</p>
