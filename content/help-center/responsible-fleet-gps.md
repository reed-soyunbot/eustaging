<p>Follow these steps to connect your Responsible Fleet GPS account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Responsible Fleet GPS account</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Account</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Users</strong></li>
  <li>Create a new user with the following settings:
    <ul>
      <li>Grant access to <strong>all vehicles</strong></li>
      <li>Grant access to <strong>all drivers</strong></li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Share the new user credentials with TruckerCloud via the onboarding widget</li>
</ol>
