<p>If you see the error <b>"Carrier Not Found"</b> on the first onboarding screen after entering your MC or DOT, follow the steps below.</p>
<h2>Why this happens</h2>
<p>This error means your MC or DOT number is not yet recognized as an active entity in our database. It happens most often with new ventures whose registration has not propagated to public carrier databases.</p>
<h2>How to fix it</h2>
<ol><li>If you entered your <b>MC number</b>, switch to your <b>DOT number</b> instead. DOT numbers tend to be more reliable.</li><li>If neither MC nor DOT works, enter your <b>PolicyID or QuoteID</b> instead (only available if your invitation includes that option).</li><li>If you do not have a PolicyID or QuoteID option, contact TruckerCloud support so we can add your DOT to our database manually.</li></ol>
<h2>Still stuck?</h2>
<p>Reach out via the chat icon in the bottom-right of your screen or email <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a> and we will add your carrier manually.</p>
