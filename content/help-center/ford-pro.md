<p>Follow these steps to connect your Ford Pro account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to the Ford Pro Developer Portal</li>
  <li>Enter your admin username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Credentials Management</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Create New Service Account</strong></li>
  <li>Name the Client ID <strong>Insurance</strong> and the Client Secret <strong>Insurance</strong>, then click <strong>Confirm</strong></li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the Client ID and paste it in the Client ID field</li>
  <li>Copy the Client Secret and paste it in the Client Secret field</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
