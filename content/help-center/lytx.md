<p>Lytx requires an authorization form before connecting. Follow these steps:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Have the account administrator sign the <a href="https://na4.docusign.net/Member/PowerFormSigning.aspx?PowerFormId=e63f6a90-cfaa-4062-a3a3-104d4f506a36&env=na4&acct=dcc58713-002d-4605-86a9-9be5e3f4bf55&v=2">Lytx data authorization form</a></li>
  <li>TruckerCloud will send the document to Lytx with your email on copy</li>
  <li>Once TruckerCloud receives your credentials from Lytx, we will complete the onboarding process for you</li>
  <li>You will receive an onboarding confirmation email</li>
</ol>
