<p>Follow these steps to connect your Azuga account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Azuga admin account</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Admin</strong>, then <strong>Users</strong>, then <strong>Add User</strong></li>
  <li>Create a new user with the following settings:
    <ul>
      <li>First Name: <strong>Insurance</strong></li>
      <li>Last Name: <strong>Company</strong></li>
      <li>Username: <strong>Insurance</strong></li>
      <li>Email: insurance@[yourcompany].com</li>
      <li>Password: choose a password</li>
      <li>User Role: <strong>Admin</strong></li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Click <strong>Save</strong></li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and enter the new credentials</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
