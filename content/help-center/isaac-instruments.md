<p>Follow these steps to connect your ISAAC Instruments account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Contact your ISAAC customer service manager or technical support</li>
  <li>In your email:
    <ul>
      <li>Request to subscribe to the API server</li>
      <li>Mention that you are working with TruckerCloud</li>
      <li>CC <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a></li>
      <li>Include that you need all API calls</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>ISAAC will create and send you the credentials (scopes/username/password/API key)</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the credentials</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Complete Sign Up</strong></li>
</ol>
