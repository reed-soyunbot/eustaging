<p>Follow these steps to connect your Fuel Express account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Fuel Express account</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Account</strong>, then click <strong>Users</strong></li>
  <li>Create a new user and fill in the required information</li>
  <li>Edit the newly created user to assign <strong>ALL VEHICLES</strong> and <strong>ALL DRIVERS</strong></li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Authorize</strong> and enter the new username and password</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
