<p>TruckerCloud aggregates data from over 130 telematics service providers (TSPs) and makes it available to 50+ insurance customers. This FAQ covers how the integration process works for new ELD and telematics providers.</p>
<h2>How do I request a new integration?</h2>
<p>Reach out to <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a>. Our development roadmap is driven primarily by customer requests. Please share your API documentation and test credentials as early as possible.</p>
<h2>What does the integration fee cost?</h2>
<p>We offer a one-time expedited integration for a flat fee of $10,000. This secures a dedicated integration slot and guarantees completion within 4-8 weeks, contingent on meeting our technical requirements.</p>
<h2>Can I get integrated without paying the fee?</h2>
<p>Without the expedited fee, your integration goes onto our standard backlog of 50+ providers. We prioritize based on customer demand and revenue potential.</p>
<h2>What technical requirements does TruckerCloud need?</h2>
<p>We require complete API documentation, API credentials, and live data access to: authentication, vehicles list/details, drivers list/details, and real-time location data.</p>
<h2>How long does the integration take?</h2>
<p>Once payment is received and all technical requirements are met, expect <b>4-8 weeks</b> from payment.</p>
<h2>What if my ELD provider requires a data sharing agreement?</h2>
<p>Some providers (like Omnitracs/Solera and Lytx) require a signed data sharing authorization before generating API credentials. The fleet signs the authorization, TruckerCloud forwards it to the ELD provider, and we wait for the credentials.</p>
