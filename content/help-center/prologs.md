<p>Follow these steps to connect your ProLogs account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your ProLogs account</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Administration</strong> on the left sidebar</li>
  <li>Click <strong>API Clients</strong></li>
  <li>Click the <strong>+</strong> button in the top right corner</li>
  <li>Fill in the <strong>Name</strong> and select the <strong>Company</strong></li>
  <li>Click the <strong>+</strong> next to Keys to generate your API Key (Client Secret)</li>
  <li>Optionally add a description and expiration date</li>
  <li>Before clicking <strong>Save</strong>, copy and save both your <strong>Client ID</strong> and <strong>API Key (Client Secret)</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Save</strong></li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and enter the Client ID and Client Secret</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
