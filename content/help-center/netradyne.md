<p>Follow these steps to connect your Netradyne account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Netradyne IDMS admin account at <a href="https://idms.netradyne.com/console/#/login">https://idms.netradyne.com/console/#/login</a></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Driveri Store</strong> from the home page (left toolbar)</li>
  <li>Search for your insurance provider</li>
  <li>Click the <strong>Integration Button</strong> (at $0.00 cost) for the company you're connecting with</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Subscribe</strong> and complete the $0 checkout</li>
  <li>From <strong>Provisioning &amp; Configurations</strong>, click <strong>Integrations</strong> — your integration will appear with an <strong>Enable</strong> option</li>
  <li>Accept the terms and conditions and provide an electronic signature to complete the data share</li>
  <li>Click <strong>API Access</strong> and copy the <strong>Fleet Unique Identifier</strong></li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste your Fleet Unique Identifier in the <strong>Tenant Unique Name</strong> field</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
