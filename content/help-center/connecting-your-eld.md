<h2>Before you start</h2>
<p>You'll need your ELD provider login credentials (the same ones you use to log into your ELD's app or web portal). You do <strong>not</strong> need to install any new hardware.</p>

<h2>Step-by-step</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Visit your insurance company's TruckerCloud program page (your insurer will provide this link).</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Connect Your Device</strong>.</li>
  <li>Select your ELD or camera provider from the list.</li>
  <li>Enter your ELD provider credentials when prompted.</li>
  <li>Confirm the vehicles you want to share data for.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Authorize</strong>. You'll see a confirmation screen when the connection is successful.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Supported providers</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud supports 170+ ELD and camera systems. Visit your program page to see the full list of supported devices. Common providers include Samsara, Motive, Geotab, Verizon Connect, and many others.</p>

<h2>Troubleshooting</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Wrong credentials</strong> — Use the credentials for your ELD provider's portal, not your TruckerCloud login.</li>
  <li><strong>Provider not listed</strong> — Contact your insurer or <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a>. We may be able to add your provider.</li>
  <li><strong>Connection succeeded but no data</strong> — It can take up to 24 hours for historical data to sync. Live data appears within a few hours of your first trip after connecting.</li>
</ul>
