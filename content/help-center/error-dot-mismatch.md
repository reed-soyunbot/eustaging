<p>If you see a <b>"DOT mismatch"</b> error during Motive or Samsara onboarding, follow the steps below.</p>
<h2>Why this happens</h2>
<p>The DOT number on your Motive or Samsara account does not match the DOT you entered on the first TruckerCloud onboarding screen.</p>
<h2>Option A — Restart with the correct DOT</h2>
<ol><li>Back out and start over from the invitation link.</li><li>Enter the DOT that matches your Motive/Samsara account.</li><li>Because your email was used previously, add a +1 to your email (e.g. <code>support+1@truckercloud.com</code>) to bypass the "Contact Email is Invited" error.</li></ol>
<h2>Option B — Update the DOT in your Motive/Samsara account</h2>
<ol><li>In Motive or Samsara, go to <b>Settings &rarr; Compliance</b>.</li><li>Update the DOT to match what you entered in onboarding.</li><li>For Motive, allow up to <b>24 hours</b> for the change to propagate before retrying.</li></ol>
<h2>Still stuck?</h2>
<p>Contact <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a> with both DOT values and we will help reconcile.</p>
