<h2>Steps to Connect</h2>
<ol><li>Log into your Linxup account.</li><li>Navigate to <b>Setup</b> at the top.</li><li>In the left sidebar, scroll down and click <b>API/Developer</b>.</li><li>Navigate to <b>V3</b> in the top right corner.</li><li>Click <b>Create New API Token</b> and copy the token.</li><li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding screen and click <b>Authorize</b>.</li><li>Paste the token and click <b>Verify Credentials</b>, then <b>Done</b>.</li></ol>
