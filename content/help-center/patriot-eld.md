<p>Follow these steps to connect your Patriot ELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>In the TruckerCloud onboarding widget, click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Enter the DOT number associated with your Patriot ELD account</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
