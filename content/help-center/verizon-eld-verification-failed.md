<p>If you authenticated your Verizon Connect Reveal account using credentials emailed by Verizon and saw <b>"Some ELD verification failed"</b>, follow the steps below.</p>
<h2>Why this happens</h2>
<p>Every vehicle and driver in your Verizon account must have <b>both a Name and a Number</b> populated, with <b>no special characters</b> in either field. If any record is missing a value or contains a special character, verification fails for the whole account.</p>
<h2>How to fix it</h2>
<ol><li>Log into your Verizon Connect Reveal account.</li><li>Click your user button (top-right) &rarr; <b>Admin &rarr; Vehicle List</b>.</li><li>Confirm every vehicle has values in both <b>Vehicle Name</b> and <b>Vehicle #</b>, and remove any special characters.</li><li>Repeat for <b>Driver List</b> — every driver must have <b>Driver Name</b> and <b>Driver #</b>.</li><li>Save changes and retry onboarding in TruckerCloud.</li></ol>
<h2>Still stuck?</h2>
<p>Contact <a href="mailto:support@truckercloud.com">support@truckercloud.com</a> with your Verizon account ID and we will help identify the failing record.</p>
