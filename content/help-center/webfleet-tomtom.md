<p>Follow these steps to connect your Webfleet / TomTom account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Contact your WebFleet representative and request an API access username and password</li>
  <li>Once you receive credentials, return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
  <li>Enter your API access username and password in the required fields</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Complete Sign Up</strong></li>
</ol>
