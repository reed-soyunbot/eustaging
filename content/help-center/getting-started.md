<h2>What is TruckerCloud?</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud is a turnkey telematics platform for commercial auto insurers. We connect to 170+ ELD and camera systems so your policyholders can share driving data with you — without switching devices.</p>

<h2>How a program works</h2>
<p>Each insurance partner gets a dedicated onboarding page (e.g. <code>truckercloud.com/program/your-program</code>). Policyholders visit that page, connect their existing ELD or camera, and data starts flowing to your dashboard.</p>

<ol>
  <li><strong>You launch</strong> — we configure your program page with your branding and the list of supported devices.</li>
  <li><strong>Policyholders onboard</strong> — they visit your program page and connect their ELD in under 5 minutes.</li>
  <li><strong>You get data</strong> — mileage, risk scores, FNOL alerts, and behavioral analytics appear in your dashboard or via API.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Key concepts</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Program page</strong> — your branded onboarding URL for policyholders</li>
  <li><strong>ELD / Camera</strong> — the device on the truck; we connect to 170+ providers</li>
  <li><strong>Risk Score</strong> — a 0–100 composite score based on speed, harsh events, and geographic risk</li>
  <li><strong>FNOL</strong> — First Notice of Loss; an automatic alert when a crash is detected</li>
</ul>
