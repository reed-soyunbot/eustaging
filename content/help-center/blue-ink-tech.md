<p>Follow these steps to connect your Blue Ink Tech (BIT) account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your Blue Ink Tech account at <a href="https://blueinktech.com/login.php">https://blueinktech.com/login.php</a></li>
  <li>Navigate to the <strong>My Account</strong> page</li>
  <li>Under the <strong>My API Keys</strong> section, click <strong>Request API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Select <strong>Read-Only</strong> permissions</li>
  <li>Copy the API key</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the key</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
