<p>Follow these steps to connect your FleetHunt account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your FleetHunt account</li>
  <li>Navigate to <strong>Manage</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>API Keys</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Generate API Key</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the generated API key</li>
  <li>Paste it into the TruckerCloud onboarding widget</li>
</ol>
