title: "Data Availability by Telematics Service Provider (TSP)"
description: "How much and what type of data is available for each TSP? (ELD and Camera)"
category: "Getting Started"
order: 30

<p class="no-margin">TruckerCloud integrates with 100+ ELD and telematics providers through a single API, but the depth of data available varies by provider. This guide outlines what data you can expect from each TSP — core ELD/GPS data, mileage capabilities, and camera detection — so you can make informed decisions about coverage, scoring, and monitoring.</p><p class="no-margin"></p>
<hr><p class="no-margin"></p>
<h1 id="h_18f4f10918">How to Read This Guide</h1><p class="no-margin"></p>
<div class="intercom-interblocks-table-container"><table role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td><p class="no-margin"><b>Column</b></p></td><td><p class="no-margin"><b>What It Means</b></p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Pre-Bind Scoring</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">TruckerCloud can generate a risk score using this TSP's data <i>before</i> a policy is bound, giving underwriters actionable insight during the quoting process.</p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Alert Monitoring</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">TruckerCloud actively monitors safety events from this TSP and surfaces alerts in real time.</p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Location</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">Real-time GPS coordinates and positioning data for vehicles in the fleet.</p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Vehicle</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">Fleet roster information — VIN, unit number, make/model, equipment details.</p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Driver</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">Driver profile information including name, license details, and vehicle assignment.</p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Historical</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">Historical breadcrumb/GPS trail data for trend analysis and retrospective risk evaluation.</p></td></tr><tr><td><p class="no-margin">Safety Events</p></td><td><p class="no-margin">Driver behavior events from the ELD or GPS tracker: hard braking, cornering, hard acceleration, and crash / potential crash. <h1 id="h_e746d8de81">Camera Detection by TSP</h1><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Not every telematics provider exposes camera hardware the same way. TruckerCloud uses one of three detection methods depending on how the TSP reports camera data.</p><p class="no-margin"></p>
<h2 id="h_3b5810e423">1. Camera by default (13)</h2><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">Camera-only providers. Every vehicle reporting through them is treated as camera-equipped, because the platform itself only exists to deliver camera data.</p><p class="no-margin"></p>
<ul><li><p class="no-margin">Eyeride</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Intellishift Driver-i Camera</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Lightmetrics</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Lytx</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Netradyne</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Pedigree Camera</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">PowerFleet</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Prometheus</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">SmartDrive</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">SmartDrive Protect</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Surfsight</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Teletrac Navman Camera</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">TXT DashCam</p></li></ul><p class="no-margin"></p>
<h2 id="h_f98dc6105a">2. Camera flagged on the assets endpoint (8)</h2><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">The vehicle list returned by these TSPs includes hardware information. We read camera presence directly from the vehicle record at the time of sync — no inference needed.</p><p class="no-margin"></p>
<ul><li><p class="no-margin">Azuga</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">FleetHunt</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Linxup</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">OneStepGPS</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Raven</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Samsara</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">Track Your Truck</p></li><li><p class="no-margin">TruckX</p></li></ul><p class="no-margin"></p>
<h2 id="h_78ef7fa435">3. Inferred from safety events (30-day lookback) (2)</h2><p class="no-margin"></p>
<p class="no-margin">These TSPs don't expose camera hardware directly on the vehicle record. Instead, we review the last 30 days of safety events for each vehicle. If any camera-generated events are present, the vehicle is flagged as camera-equipped. A vehicle with a camera but no recent safety events during the lookback window may not be flagged until events start coming through.</p><p class="no-margin"></p>
