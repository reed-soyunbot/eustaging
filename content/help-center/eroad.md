<p>Follow these steps to connect your ERoad account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your EROAD portal at <a href="https://depot.eroad.com/Portal/login.jsp">https://depot.eroad.com/Portal/login.jsp</a></li>
  <li>Click the <strong>Admin</strong> button (gear icon at top)</li>
  <li>Click <strong>My Organization</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Add</strong></li>
  <li>Enter the gateway partner name (<strong>Canal</strong>) and Group Name (<strong>TC Group</strong>)</li>
  <li>Enable receiving data via the Partner Gateway</li>
  <li>Copy the generated key</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the key</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
