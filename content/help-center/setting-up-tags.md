<p>Tags let you group carriers in your TruckerCloud account by a shared attribute — for example, by product line — so you can filter, segment, and report on them together.</p>
<h2>Creating a Tag</h2>
<ol><li>Navigate to <b>Settings</b>.</li><li>Go to <b>Manage Tags</b>.</li><li>Click <b>Create Tag</b>.</li><li>Select a <b>Category</b> (currently only <b>Product Line</b> is available).</li><li>Enter a <b>Tag Name</b> and click <b>Save</b>.</li></ol>
<h2>Assigning Carriers to a Tag</h2>
<p>After a tag is created, assign it to one or more carriers. Those fleets will appear under that tag and can be filtered by it throughout the platform.</p>
<h2>Removing Carriers from a Tag</h2>
<ol><li>Go to <b>Settings &gt; Manage Tags</b> and open the tag.</li><li>Select the carriers you want to remove.</li><li>Click <b>Unassign</b>.</li></ol>
<p>The selected carriers are removed from the tag immediately. The tag itself remains available for future use.</p>
