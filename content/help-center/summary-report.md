<p>The Summary Report is the first page that opens when you click into a motor carrier in TruckerCloud. It provides a single-page snapshot of scores, mileage, active vehicles, geographic activity, and a unit-level breakdown.</p>
<h2>Exporting the Report</h2>
<p>A <b>PDF export</b> button is available at the top of the report. Use it to document a carrier's file at <b>new business</b>, <b>midterm</b>, and <b>renewal</b> for side-by-side comparison over time.</p>
<h2>How Scores Update</h2>
<ul><li>Scores are recalculated every <b>Sunday</b>.</li><li>Each calculation uses the <b>most recent 30 days of data</b>.</li><li>On each new run, the oldest 7 days drop off and the most recent 7 days are added in.</li></ul>
<h2>Active Vehicles and Mileage</h2>
<p>The KPI section shows active vehicles, total miles, and average miles per unit — all over the last 30 days.</p>
<h2>Risk Score and National Average</h2>
<p>The carrier's current <b>Risk Score</b> is shown alongside the national average. Higher is better — 100 is the ideal score.</p>
<h2>Geographic Breakdowns</h2>
<p>The report includes a real-time vehicle location map, miles by state, and a heat map of operations. Activity is also viewable by zip code.</p>
<h2>Active Units (Last 90 Days)</h2>
<p>The final section is a sortable table of all units active in the last 90 days. Columns include VIN, mileage, safety events, speeding percentage, current risk score, and score change vs. last month.</p>
