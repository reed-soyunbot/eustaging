<h2>Billing structure</h2>
<p>TruckerCloud invoices on a monthly basis. Each account has two components:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Platform minimum</strong> — a fixed monthly fee that applies regardless of usage</li>
  <li><strong>Usage overage</strong> — a per-vehicle or per-mile charge applied when activity exceeds the minimum threshold</li>
</ul>

<h2>Platform minimums</h2>
<p>The platform minimum is set at contract signing and reflects your program's expected volume. You are billed the minimum even in months where actual usage falls below it.</p>

<h2>Overage billing</h2>
<p>When your connected vehicles generate activity above the minimum threshold, overage charges are calculated and billed separately. You will see two line items on your invoice: the platform fee and the overage amount.</p>

<h2>Billable vehicles</h2>
<p>A vehicle is considered billable for a given month if it recorded at least 1 GPS mile during that period. Vehicles that are connected but inactive (0 miles) are not billed as overage.</p>
