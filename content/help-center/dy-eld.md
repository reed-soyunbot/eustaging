<p>Follow these steps to connect your DY ELD account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your DY ELD account</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Manage</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Company</strong></li>
  <li>Click <strong>Edit</strong></li>
  <li>Copy your Company ID (format: <code>Company:hRY4ndJUI8</code>)</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and click <strong>Authorize</strong></li>
  <li>Paste the Company ID in the field</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
