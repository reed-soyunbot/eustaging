<p>Follow these steps to connect your GPSTab account to TruckerCloud:</p>
<ol>
  <li>Log in to your <a href="https://app.gpstab.com/">GPSTab account</a></li>
  <li>In the upper right of the portal, hover over the account dropdown and select <strong>API Tokens</strong></li>
  <li>On the right side, click <strong>Generate Token</strong></li>
  <li>In the pop-up window, type <strong>Insurance</strong> then click <strong>Generate</strong></li>
  <li>Copy the generated token</li>
  <li>Return to the TruckerCloud onboarding widget and paste the key</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Verify Credentials</strong>, then <strong>Done</strong></li>
</ol>
