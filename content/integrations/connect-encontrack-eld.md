## Location
Encontrack tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Encontrack tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Encontrack tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Encontrack tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
