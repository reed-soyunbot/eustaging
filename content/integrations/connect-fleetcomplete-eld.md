The ELD solution helps trucking companies comply with the ELD rule by recording and monitoring HOS data electronically. The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. FleetComplete ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools.
