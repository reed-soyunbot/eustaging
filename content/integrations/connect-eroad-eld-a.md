The ELD solution helps trucking companies comply with the ELD rule by electronically recording and monitoring the hours of service (HOS) of their drivers. The device tracks and reports the HOS data in real-time, providing improved compliance, increased efficiency, and reduced risk of penalties. EROAD ELD also offers additional features, such as vehicle diagnostics, driver safety, and fleet management tools.

## Location
EROAD tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
EROAD tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Driver
EROAD tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
