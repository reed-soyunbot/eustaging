The ELD solution helps trucking companies comply with the ELD rule by recording and monitoring HOS data electronically. The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. Geotab ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools to help companies better manage their fleets and drivers.

## Location
Geotab tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Geotab tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Driver
Geotab tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.

## Safety Events
Geotab tracks events such as speeding, harsh breaking, harsh acceleration, and cornering.
