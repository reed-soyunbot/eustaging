## Location
DriverTech tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
DriverTech tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
DriverTech tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
DriverTech tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
