## Location
ATT tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
ATT tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Driver
ATT tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.

## Safety Events
ATT tracks events such as speeding, harsh breaking, harsh acceleration, and cornering.

## Historical Location
ATT tracks the historical location of any asset, or truck.
