The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. Fleetmatics ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools to help companies better manage their fleets and drivers.

## Location
Fleetmatics (Verizon Connect) tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Fleetmatics (Verizon Connect) tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Fleetmatics (Verizon Connect) tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Fleetmatics (Verizon Connect) tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.

## Safety Events
Fleetmatics (Verizon Connect) tracks events such as speeding, harsh breaking, harsh acceleration, and cornering.

## Historical Location
Fleetmatics (Verizon Connect) tracks the historical location of any asset, or truck.
