The ELD solution helps trucking companies comply with the ELD rule by recording and monitoring HOS data electronically. The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. Transflo ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools to help companies better manage their fleets and drivers.

## Location
Transflo tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Transflo tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Driver
Transflo tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.

## Safety Events
Transflo tracks events such as speeding, harsh breaking, harsh acceleration, and cornering.

## Historical Location
Transflo tracks the historical location of any asset, or truck.
