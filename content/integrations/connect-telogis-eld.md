## Location
Telogis tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Telogis tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Telogis tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Telogis tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
