## Location
Trackensure tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Trackensure tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Trackensure tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Trackensure tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
