## Location
Peoplenet tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Peoplenet tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Peoplenet tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Peoplenet tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
