The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. TruckX ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools to help companies better manage their fleets and drivers.

## Location
TruckX tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
TruckX tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
TruckX tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
TruckX tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
