## Location
Samsara tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Samsara tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Samsara tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Samsara tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.

## Safety Events
Samsara tracks events such as speeding, harsh breaking, harsh acceleration, and cornering.

## Historical Location
Samsara tracks the historical location of any asset, or truck.
