eldDescription: Orbcomm ELD (Electronic Logging Device) is a device and software solution designed to meet the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) mandate for electronic logging of hours of service (HOS) for commercial truck drivers. The ELD solution helps trucking companies comply with the ELD rule by recording and monitoring HOS data electronically. The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. Orbcomm ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools to help companies better manage their fleets and drivers.
