## Location
Apollo tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Apollo tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Apollo tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Apollo tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
