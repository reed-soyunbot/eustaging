The ELD solution helps trucking companies comply with the ELD rule by recording and monitoring HOS data electronically. The device provides real-time tracking and reporting of HOS data, improving compliance, increasing efficiency, and reducing the risk of penalties. Webfleet ELD also offers additional features, such as GPS tracking, vehicle diagnostics, and fleet management tools to help companies better manage their fleets and drivers.

## Location
Webfleet tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
Webfleet tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
Webfleet tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
Webfleet tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
