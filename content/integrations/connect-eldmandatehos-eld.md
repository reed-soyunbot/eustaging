It refers to a federal regulation in the United States that requires commercial truck drivers to use an electronic logging device to track their hours of service (HOS) and ensure compliance with regulations related to rest breaks, maximum driving time, and other rules. The mandate aims to improve road safety and prevent driver fatigue.

## Location
ELDMandateHOS tracks current location of an asset, or truck.

## Vehicle
ELDMandateHOS tracks vehicle information, VIN, license plate, and calendar data.

## Hours of Service
ELDMandateHOS tracks Hours of Service, including drive shift and cycle time.

## Driver
ELDMandateHOS tracks driver information such as license number, first name, last name, contact info, etc.
